### AI News Summary - Week of October 30, 2023

1. **xAI Co-founders Depart**: This week, Elon Musk's xAI faced a significant upheaval with the resignation of two co-founders within 24 hours. This marks a troubling trend for the company, with a total of six co-founders leaving, raising concerns about its leadership and vision. xAI, established to rival OpenAI and Google, is struggling to meet performance expectations, particularly with its various AI products. Experts highlight that the ongoing departures could hinder the company's development.

2. **AI Integration in Consumer Technology**: Generative AI continues to revolutionize consumer technology by enhancing drug development, sustainable energy practices, and even complex scientific research. This progress signifies an important step towards making AI more accessible and affordable, highlighting its value in tackling global challenges.

3. **Government Step In on AI Standards**: The White House announced plans to implement standards for AI development, emphasizing the ethical and responsible integration of AI technologies. This initiative indicates a growing recognition of the need for regulation and governance in the fast-evolving AI landscape.
